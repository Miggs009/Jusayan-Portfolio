Reminders Before & During the Booking

  1. • Hygiene: Fairies are required to be clean before their shift. Clients are requested to provide bathroom access for their showering or changing (depending on what you are comfortable with). Rest assured that Fairies bring their own toiletries and clothes.

  2. • Meals and Snacks: Fairies typically bring their own meals, snacks, and cutlery. If clients wish to share a meal with them, advance notice is appreciated. That way they won't bring their packed meals. Clients are also encouraged to allow Fairies to refill their water bottles from the client's water supply.

  3. • Tipping: Tipping or gift-giving is not obligatory, but clients are welcome to do so if they are highly satisfied with the Fairies' service. Tips and gifts go directly to the Fairies.

  4. • No Phone Policy: Fairies are not allowed to use their phones while on duty. Clients are encouraged to report any violations of this policy.

  5. • Maintaining Professional Distance: Clients are requested to maintain a professional distance with Fairies, as they are independent contractors. In line with this, please avoid sharing your personal information (phone number, Facebook account details) with the Fairy and vice versa. This is to prevent over familiarity between Fairies and clients.

  6. • Transactions through the Office: All payments and bookings should be conducted strictly through the office. This is to avoid confusion and conflicts. If there are separate transactions with Fairies, the office cannot assist in case of concerns or issues.

  7. • Mask Policy: Requiring the use of masks throughout the duty is a good safety measure, with exceptions for eating or drinking. Clients are encouraged to report any violations.

  8. • Supervised interaction: Since the Fairies are not stay-in help, we would suggest that all services would be supervised by yourself or a trusted family member. This is to ensure that your child does not get scared with an unfamiliar face.
